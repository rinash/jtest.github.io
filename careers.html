Principal .NET Developer Azure, CQRS/ES, Microservices (Remote)

Lanamark, a global provider of cloud-based infrastructure analytics and design automation software, is looking for a senior .NET developer with 10+ years of experience. You will have a chance to work with the latest technologies and a super-talented team to develop the next generation of our Big Data platform for systems engineering automation.

You must be able to work independently, manage your time efficiently and communicate with a global team spread across multiple time zones. We offer excellent compensation, flexible hours and lots of vacation.

Requirements

Deep knowledge of C# and the .NET stack

Experience with domain-driven design, CQRS/ES and microservices

Passion for solving challenging problems and building scalable, high-performance applications

Desirable Skills

Full-stack development experience

Cloud-based services (Azure / AWS)

Time-Series databases (InfluxDB)

Web front-end frameworks (React)

Solid computer science / mathematics background

Location

This position is remote but preference is for candidates in Belarus, Bulgaria, Czech Republic, Poland, Romania, Russia, Serbia and Ukraine.
